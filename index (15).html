About Us

Crafted since 1994. Now stepping into India.

Lord Walker began three decades ago with a simple belief: the details you don't see are the ones you feel the most. What started as a small craftsmanship-first sock maker has grown into a name people trust for comfort that lasts an entire day, not just a photoshoot.

Today, Lord Walker brings that same premium cotton, finest craftsmanship, and timeless comfort to India — built for boardrooms, morning walks, match days, and every ordinary moment in between. Every pair is made for you, not just made to sell.
